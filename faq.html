Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

Visit the booking page, select your route and date, choose a seat, and proceed with payment.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, cancellations and modifications are allowed up to 24 hours before departure. A small processing fee may apply.

What payment options are available?

We accept debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, and mobile wallets like Paytm and PhonePe.

Will I get a physical ticket?

No, a digital ticket will be sent via email and SMS after successful booking. You can also download it from the “Booking History” page.

Is it safe to pay online?

Yes. All transactions are secured with 128-bit SSL encryption to protect your payment details.
